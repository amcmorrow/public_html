Write an extra gooey monkey bread recipe
Ingredients:
- 3 cans of refrigerated biscuits
- 1 cup of brown sugar
- 1 cup of white sugar
- 2 sticks of unsalted butter
- 1 tablespoon of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
- 1 cup of chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.

2. Cut each biscuit into quarters and set aside.

3. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.

4. Add the brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla extract to the melted butter. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.

5. Add the chopped nuts to the butter mixture (if using).

6. Grease a bundt pan with cooking spray.

7. Dip each biscuit quarter into the butter mixture and place it in the bundt pan.

8. Pour any remaining butter mixture over the biscuits.

9. Bake the monkey bread for 35-40 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the bread is cooked through.

10. Let the monkey bread cool for 5 minutes before flipping it onto a serving plate.

11. Serve warm and enjoy the gooey goodness!
